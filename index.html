ABOUT US

We Love Aural is a rotation curation social media account for lovers of music everywhere. Since our launch in December 2013 a new host has taken us on a musical journey each week. Whether it's every song they've ever loved from their birth to present day, a collection of meaningful tunes and clips or even a deep look at a particular genre, each one has brought something different, something wonderful. Here at WLA we know music means something different to everyone. We also know music means something to everyone. And that's why we love aural (heh)
